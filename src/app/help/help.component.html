Add Spending by Mail

Here are the steps you need to follow in order to add a spending by mail

  1. Sign into your Mail Account, using your Finance-Tracker Account Email address
  2. Set the receiver to fnctracker@gmail.com
  3. Write the spending description as the subject
  4. Write 'amount: xy CHF' in the email body (xy is your desired amount)
  5. Send the email
